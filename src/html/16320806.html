About me & my future projects:

Couple of months ago, I have always had this invisible motivation that felt is there but never been able to do something with it. It's like I ready for the next step yet something was holding me back...


Couple of events later; after seeing some people receiving fame that they do not deserve, I finally decided that I've had enough! I am ready to make a change and thats why I am working on some projects on both sides of Music and Programming.


These projects are the start of something big, an artistic revolution for our country, something never seen before! It takes time and effort and a lot of energy & motive.


Here is a little story, I am someone who always loved music and the making of it! It inspires me how beautiful it is. Throughout time I met some people who helped me out to find other people that feel the same thing about music.


After some time, I knew that having fame and money will make you feel isolated and lost, make your life seem like it is meaningless. That's the disadvantage of loving and making music.


My skills in music production grow better day after day, you have to love music in order to make good music; not like some other people that do it for money and fame. Not my thing.


One thing about me is that I hate fame, it comes with a lot of problems. People think that another persons knowing your name is the coolest thing ever, I don't think it's the case.


Influence is like having a big responsibility, I remember I had a classmate that'd usually sit with my at lunchtime, then I would take out a cigarette and light it. Day after day, he started asking for puffs, which is something bad, obviously. I never gave him the chance to smoke with me yet 2 months later he started smoking double the amount I smoke.


Long story short, "Fame", "Money" & "Influence" are like my worst nightmares, so to my future self: BEWARE THOSE THINGS.
